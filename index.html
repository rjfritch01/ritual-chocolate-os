The Opportunity

Built for how Ritual actually works

Right now, critical business information lives in spreadsheets, paper logs, and people's heads. As you scale to 10,000 lbs a month, that creates real risk.

📋
Production is invisible
No single place to see where all four batches are in the process, who's done what, or what's been signed off on.
📦
Inventory is scattered
Stock in the café, warehouse, tour room, and samples has no unified view — leading to over-ordering, stockouts, and waste.
🤝
Sales relationships are fragile
Wholesale buyers, distributors, affiliates, and Shopify customers are tracked differently or not at all — critical relationships can go cold.
👤
Every role works in isolation
Chocolatiers, QC, packaging, and warehouse all need different views. Generic tools don't support role-specific workflows.
Before vs. After
Before
Batch status lives in Marcus's memory and a shared Google Sheet
After
Live dashboard — any team member sees exactly where all 4 batches are in real time
Before
Café calls warehouse to ask if Bourbon Barrel is in stock
After
One screen shows all 24 SKUs across every location with low-stock alerts
Before
Zingerman's goes 90 days without an order and nobody notices
After
Aging flag surfaces after 30 days — a reminder to reach out before the relationship goes cold
Before
Roast times and temperature readings go unrecorded
After
Built-in timers and stage logs capture every reading with timestamps and staff sign-offs

Why a Custom System

Off-the-shelf software doesn't make chocolate

Generic tools like Salesforce, Monday.com, or spreadsheets require Ritual to adapt its process to fit the software. This is the opposite — software built around the way Ritual already works.

🎯
Built for your workflow
Every screen, every field, every process reflects how Ritual actually makes chocolate — not how a generic SaaS company thinks you should. No configuration. No workarounds.
👥
Every role, one system
Marcus sees his chocolatier checklist. Sofia sees her QC stages. Dana sees everything. One system with role-specific views means nobody sees irrelevant information — and nobody misses what matters.
📈
Grows with Ritual
Start with production, inventory, and sales. Add reporting, mobile access, and integrations as the business grows. Built to expand, not to be replaced when you outgrow it.
